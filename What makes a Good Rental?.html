Current Market Analysis

Rental Rates per Bedroom: $650 - $950/month depending on location and condition
Optimal Property Size: 4-6 bedrooms generating $2,600-$4,200/month gross rent

Two Proven Investment Strategies

Cash Flow Focused

  • Immediate monthly returns (8-12% cap rates)
  • Move-in ready condition
  • $650-$750 per bedroom range
  • Lower maintenance, passive management

Appreciation Focused

  • Premium locations with growth potential
  • Properties suitable for improvements
  • Higher long-term returns
  • More active management approach

Essential Property Characteristics

Must-Have Features

  • 4-6 bedrooms (optimal rental yield)
  • Minimum 2 full bathrooms
  • Adequate parking (street parking acceptable)
  • Functional kitchen and common areas
  • Laundry facilities (in-unit strongly preferred)

Value-Add Opportunities

  • Basement conversion potential
  • Properties needing cosmetic updates
  • Energy efficiency improvements
  • Modern amenity additions
  • Outdoor space enhancements
Investment Sweet Spot: Look for 4-5 bedroom properties that can generate $650-$850 per bedroom monthly while offering potential for value improvements or strategic positioning near campus growth areas.
