Why Listings Expire

And How We Turn Expired into Executed
Most expired listings aren't unsellable. They just need a new story, a better strategy, and the right person driving the process.

The Three Main Culprits

1. Overpricing Without Perspective

The Problem: Today's buyers are informed and quick to pass on listings they feel are overpriced, especially without clear justification.
The Solution: I offer pricing strategies aligned with your goals—whether that's speed, top dollar, or maximum exposure. Using comps, local trends, and buyer behavior, we price your home where it will perform, not just sit.

2. Misaligned Strategy

The Problem: Not every home needs to be polished to perfection, but every listing needs a plan that matches the seller's goals.
The Solution: I tailor the listing strategy to the property and the seller—not a one-size-fits-all checklist. Whether we're maximizing appeal or streamlining for speed, the way your home shows aligns with what you're trying to achieve.

3. Passive Marketing

The Problem: Putting a home on the MLS is not a marketing plan. If the right buyers didn't know your home was available, the listing went stale.
The Solution: I market with intent—professional media, compelling positioning, and direct outreach to agents with active buyers. We create demand instead of waiting for it.

Re-List Roadmap

How we turn Expired into Executed
1
Expired Listing Evaluation
I'll tour your home and review what went wrong the first time—from pricing and presentation to marketing and buyer feedback. This helps us build a fresh strategy that addresses past obstacles.
2
Representation Agreement
Once we align on the new plan, we execute a listing agreement so I can officially represent the property and begin attracting the strongest, most qualified offer the market will bear.
3
Strategic Property Positioning
We'll reset pricing based on recent comps, neighborhood trends, and your selling goals. I'll identify your most likely buyer and tailor our messaging to highlight what matters most to them.
4
Marketing Refresh & Online Presence
We'll update photography (if needed), rewrite the listing copy, and relaunch with stronger visuals and a clearer story. If helpful, we can add floorplans, drone footage, or additional staging.
5
Targeted Outreach Begins
I don't just wait for the MLS to do the work—I proactively reach out to agents with active buyers, local networks, and personal contacts to generate real interest.
6
Ongoing Feedback & Adjustment
You'll receive regular updates on showings, buyer feedback, and market activity. If needed, we'll make timely adjustments to keep momentum strong and drive results.
