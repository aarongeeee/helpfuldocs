Remote Worker's Real Estate Playbook

Maximize Tax Savings & Property Value in Eugene
Quick Win: Average remote worker in Oregon saves $2,500-4,200 annually with proper home office deductions. Most miss 60% of eligible deductions.

Tax Deduction Strategies

Simplified Method

  • $5 per sq ft (max 300 sq ft)
  • Maximum $1,500 deduction
  • Minimal record keeping

Actual Expense Method

  • Deduct actual home expenses
  • Typically saves $2,000+ more
  • Requires detailed records
Eugene Example: 150 sq ft office in $425k home = ~$3,600 annual deduction

Often Missed Deductions

  • $Internet & Phone: Business use percentage (50-80%)
  • $Office Furniture: Desks, chairs, storage
  • $Home Improvements: Office renovations
  • $Utilities: Equipment electricity, heating/cooling
  • $Software: Zoom, project management tools
